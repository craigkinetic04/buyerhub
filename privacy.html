Privacy Policy

Effective: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Closing Day ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. Closing Day is provided by Craig Rogers.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly. When you sign up for or are invited to Closing Day, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (optional)
  • Your password (stored securely as a one-way hash; we never see your password in plain text)

Information you create within the App. As you use Closing Day, you may generate additional data, including:

  • Homebuying checklist progress and completion timestamps
  • Real estate listings you favorite or save
  • Notes and preferences you share with your real estate agent or loan professional
  • Requests to be contacted by your loan professional

Information about your real estate professional. If you are a real estate agent or loan professional using Closing Day, we collect your name, email, phone number, professional affiliation, and the data you create about your clients (with their consent).

Technical information. Like most apps, we automatically collect certain technical information, including device type, operating system version, and IP address. This is used to keep the app secure and functioning correctly.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the core functionality of the App, including account access and saving your progress
  • Connect you with your real estate agent or loan professional
  • Send you transactional emails (such as account invites, password resets, or service updates)
  • Improve the App's features and reliability
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your information for advertising. We do not share your information with third-party advertisers.

How We Share Your Information

With your real estate professional. If you are a homebuyer, the real estate agent or loan professional who invited you can see the data you create within the App (your checklist progress, favorited listings, and any contact requests). This is the core purpose of the App — to keep you and your professional aligned.

With trusted service providers. We use the following third-party services to operate the App. Each is bound by strict data handling requirements:

  • Supabase — secure cloud database and authentication. supabase.com/privacy
  • Resend — transactional email delivery. resend.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Apple — iOS app distribution and analytics. apple.com/legal/privacy

When required by law. We may disclose information if required to do so by valid legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of any person.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest, secure password hashing, and access controls that limit who can view your data.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at craig@kinetic-mgmt.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Closing Day is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the App or by email. Continued use of Closing Day after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
