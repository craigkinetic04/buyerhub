Step 1 of 9
Generate a Partner Link
This is the first thing you do when onboarding a new REALTOR® partner. You go to your Partner Generator, enter their info, and it creates their personalized CRM link and writes their outreach message for you.
What This Tool Does
  • 1
    You enter the REALTOR®'s name, company, phone, and email
    The generator creates a unique ID for them and builds their personalized CRM link with their info already embedded.
  • 2
    A pre-written text message and email are generated
    Both include the CRM link and explain what the tool does. You just tap Send — no writing required.
  • 3
    The REALTOR® opens their link and creates an account
    They set up a password on their first visit. After that they log in from any device with their email and password.
Try it now: Open the Partner Generator in a new tab and enter test info — use your own name and email so you can follow the REALTOR® experience in the next steps.
Open Partner Generator ↗
Step 2 of 9
Log Into the CRM as a REALTOR®
Now you're going to experience the CRM the way your REALTOR® partners do. Open the CRM link and create a test account — or log in if you already have one.
What the REALTOR® Sees When They Log In
  • 1
    First visit — they create a password
    The CRM asks for their name, email, and a password. After this they log in automatically for 30 days — no re-entering credentials.
  • 2
    They land on their dashboard
    Their personalized dashboard shows stat cards (active clients, follow-ups due, under contract, closed), a client list, and today's pending workflow emails.
  • 3
    Three tabs in the header
    My Clients — their CRM. Buyer Hub — their hub setup and buyer link. Templates — all pre-written emails.
Test mode available: On the CRM page, type the word testmode on your keyboard (no field needed — just type it anywhere on the page). A password prompt appears — enter Laqu1sha!1 to enter test mode with fake data. Nothing saves to Airtable in test mode.
Open the CRM ↗
Step 3 of 9
Set Up the Buyer Hub Profile
Inside the CRM, click the Buyer Hub tab in the header. This is where the REALTOR® customizes their hub before sharing it with buyers.
What to Fill In
  • 1
    Profile — name, company, phone, email, headshot
    Upload a headshot by clicking the Upload Photo button. The photo saves to the database and shows at the top of every buyer's hub automatically.
  • 2
    Featured Listings — homes you want buyers to see
    Click + Add Listing to add properties. Any buyer who opens the hub will see these listings on the Find Properties tab.
  • 3
    Save Profile to generate the buyer link
    Once saved, the buyer link appears with three buttons — Copy Link, Send via Text, and Send via Email — all pre-written and ready to go.
Important: The buyer link is generated from the REALTOR®'s profile. Every buyer who opens it sees the REALTOR®'s photo and contact info on every page. The link is the same for all buyers — each person's checklist progress is private to them.
Open CRM → Buyer Hub Tab ↗
Step 4 of 9
Add a Test Client
Go to the My Clients tab and add a test client. Use a real email address you have access to — you'll need it to experience the buyer side in the next steps.
How to Add a Client
  • 1
    Click + Add Client in the sidebar or header
    A form slides open. Fill in their name, email (use one you can access), phone, and stage. Leave other fields blank for now.
  • 2
    Click Save
    The client appears in your pipeline list immediately and saves to Airtable. You can now click on them to open their full profile.
  • 3
    Explore the 5 tabs in their profile
    Info — contact details. Checklist — their hub progress. Workflow — follow-up emails. Share Hub — send their buyer link. Emails — manual templates.
Use your own email as the test client's email. This way when you open the buyer link in the next step, the system will recognize you and your checklist progress will show in the REALTOR®'s CRM view.
Open CRM → Add Client ↗
Step 5 of 9
Send the Buyer Hub Link
Now you'll send your test client their personalized buyer hub link. This is exactly what a REALTOR® does when they get a new buyer.
How to Share the Buyer Hub
  • 1
    Open the test client you just created
    Click on their name in the client list to open their profile panel.
  • 2
    Click the Share Hub tab
    You'll see a card with the buyer's personalized link and three options — Copy, Text, or Email. The link has their name and email baked in so they don't need to log in.
  • 3
    Copy the buyer link
    Copy the link and save it somewhere — you'll open it in the next step to experience what the buyer sees. The link contains buyerName and buyerEmail already filled in.
What makes this link special: The buyer's name and email are embedded in the URL. When they open it they land directly in their hub — no login, no form, no friction. Their progress saves automatically to Airtable and shows in your CRM Checklist tab.
Open CRM → Share Hub Tab ↗
Step 6 of 9
Experience the Buyer's Side
Open the buyer link you copied in the last step. This is exactly what your buyers see when you send them their hub. Check off several items on the checklist so you can verify the progress shows in the CRM.
What the Buyer Sees
  • 1
    They land directly in their hub — no login needed
    Their name appears in the header ("Hi, [Name] •"). The REALTOR®'s photo and contact info shows at the top. They are ready to go immediately.
  • 2
    Buyer Checklist tab — 46 steps across 7 sections
    From checking credit scores all the way to getting keys. Each section collapses and expands. Progress percentage shows at the top. Check off 5–10 items to test.
  • 3
    Find Properties tab — Oklahoma home search
    Launches Zillow, Realtor.com, or Redfin with city and price filters pre-applied. REALTOR®'s featured listings show here too.
  • 4
    Progress saves to Airtable after 1.5 seconds
    When a buyer checks off a step, "✓ Progress saved" appears. Their progress syncs to Airtable and will show in the REALTOR®'s CRM checklist view.
Make sure you check off several items before going to the next step — you need saved progress in Airtable for the CRM checklist test to work.
Open Buyer Hub ↗
Step 7 of 9
See the Checklist in the CRM
Go back to the CRM and open your test client. Click the Checklist tab — you should see the items you just checked off in the Buyer Hub reflected here in real time.
What the REALTOR® Sees
  • 1
    All 46 steps organized by section
    Get Your Finances in Order, Gather Your Documents, Explore Down Payment Assistance, Get Pre-Approved, Find Your Home, Make an Offer, and Closing Day.
  • 2
    Completed steps shown with gold checkmarks
    Every item the buyer has checked off shows as complete. The section header shows "X/Y" so the REALTOR® can see progress at a glance.
  • 3
    Progress bar with percentage
    A gold progress bar at the top of the checklist tab shows overall completion percentage. Updates live as the buyer checks things off.
If you don't see progress: Wait 5–10 seconds and refresh the CRM page. The data syncs from Airtable on page load. Make sure the test client's email matches exactly what you used in the buyer hub.
Open CRM → Checklist Tab ↗
Step 8 of 9
Test Workflows & Email Templates
The workflow system is the automation engine of the CRM. Assign a workflow to your test client and explore the pre-written emails that go out automatically.
The 6 Workflows Available
First-Time Buyer 6–12 Months
7 emails over 120 days
Ready to Buy Now
5 emails over 14 days
Past Client Nurture
5 emails over 365 days
Seller Lead
5 emails over 30 days
Not Ready 12+ Months
7 emails over 365 days
Holiday Touch-Base
2 emails, seasonal
How to Test a Workflow
  • 1
    Open the test client → Workflow tab
    Select "Ready to Buy Now" from the dropdown — it's the shortest workflow with 5 steps over 14 days. Click Assign This Workflow.
  • 2
    See the scheduled emails appear
    All 5 emails show with their due dates. Click Send Email on any step to open it in your email app — the subject and body are already written with the client's name filled in.
  • 3
    Connect your email for automatic sending
    Click Connect Email in the sidebar. The 4-step wizard connects your Gmail or Outlook through EmailJS. After connecting, due emails fire automatically every morning when you open the CRM.
  • 4
    Check the Templates tab for standalone emails
    Click the Templates tab in the header to see all 9 standalone email templates — new client welcome, pre-approval reminder, offer congratulations, closing day, and more. Each sends in one click.
Every email includes an unsubscribe footer. If a client replies UNSUBSCRIBE, the REALTOR® toggles the Unsubscribed switch in the client's Info tab — all emails to that client are then blocked automatically.
Open CRM → Workflow Tab ↗
