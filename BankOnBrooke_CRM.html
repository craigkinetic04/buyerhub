Help & FAQ

Getting Started
How do I add a client?
▾
Click + Add Client in the sidebar or the top right of the client list. Fill in their name, email, phone, and select their pipeline stage. Click Save — they appear in your client list immediately.
What are pipeline stages?
▾
Buyer Lead — someone interested but not started. Pre-Approval — working on getting approved. Home Search — actively looking. Under Contract — offer accepted. Closed — keys in hand. Seller Lead — a listing client.
Workflows & Emails
How do workflows work?
▾
Open a client → click the Workflow tab → select a workflow → click Assign. The system schedules follow-up emails automatically. Each morning when you open the CRM, any emails due that day fire automatically from your connected email address.
How do I connect my email for automatic sending?
▾
Click Connect Email in the sidebar. The 4-step wizard connects your Gmail or Outlook through EmailJS. Takes about 2 minutes. After that, workflow emails send automatically every morning from your address — you never have to click anything.
Can I send an email manually without a workflow?
▾
Yes — open any client and click the Emails tab. Every pre-written template is there. Click Send and it opens in your email app already addressed and written. You can edit it before sending.
What happens if a client unsubscribes?
▾
Every email has an unsubscribe footer. If a client replies with UNSUBSCRIBE, open their profile → Info tab → toggle the Unsubscribed switch on. All emails to them will be blocked and a red warning shows on their card.
Buyer Hub
How do I send a buyer their hub link?
▾
Open the client → click the Share Hub tab → use the 💬 Text or ✉️ Email button. A pre-written message with their personalized link opens in your messages or email app. One tap and it sends.
How do I see what my buyer has checked off?
▾
Open the client → click the Checklist tab. You see all 46 steps organized by section with checkmarks on everything they have completed. It updates automatically as they check things off in their hub.
How do I set up my Buyer Hub profile?
▾
Click Buyer Hub in the top navigation. Fill in your name, company, phone, and email. Upload your headshot. Add your featured listings. Click Save Profile — your buyer link is generated and ready to share.
Account
Can I use this on my phone?
▾
Yes. Open your CRM link in Safari or Chrome on your phone. Tap the share button and select Add to Home Screen — it works like an app. Your login session stays active for 30 days so you stay logged in automatically.
