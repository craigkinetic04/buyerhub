How to Use Your Client CRM
Tap to expand — reference this anytime
▼
1
Add your clients
Click + Add Client to create a client profile. Fill in their name, email, phone, budget, and stage. The stage tells you where they are in the buying or selling process.
2
Assign a workflow
Open any client, scroll to the Workflow section, and pick the workflow that matches their situation — First-Time Buyer, Ready to Buy Now, Past Client, and more. Preview the steps before assigning. Once assigned, follow-up emails schedule automatically.
3
Connect your email for automatic sending
In the left sidebar click Connect My Email. The 4-step wizard walks you through connecting your Gmail or Outlook. Takes 2 minutes. After that, workflow emails send automatically from your address every morning — you never have to click anything.
4
Track progress on each client
Open any client to see their workflow progress — which emails have been sent, which are upcoming, and which are overdue. Use Mark Done to manually complete a step. Use Send Email to send a step immediately without waiting for the due date.
5
Send follow-up emails manually anytime
Open a client and scroll to Follow-Up Templates. Every pre-written email template is there. Click Send to open it in your email app pre-filled with the client's name and your signature. Use these anytime outside of the workflow schedule.
6
Use the Pipeline to stay organized
The left sidebar shows all your clients organized by stage. Click any stage to filter your view. The Overview box shows your totals at a glance. The Follow-Ups number shows clients in stages that need active attention.
